📌 Key Rules at a Glance

Fear RP — 1v1 with no weapon drawn = must fear. 3v1 or more = must fear regardless, even with a weapon out. No fear = death = CK
PD Rule — Shoot first and get head tapped = valid CK
CK Rule — Requires 6+ meaningful interactions before a CK
Brandishing — Pulling a weapon = initiation, but 2 mins RP required before shots
Plug Wars — Staff triggered only. T2+ factions only

Business Tier System

Earn points through ads & events to unlock rewards for your business

▼
How Points Work: Business owners earn 2 points per ad posted. Night Clubs earn 6 points per event hosted. Hit tier thresholds to unlock rewards. Once you unlock the Stock Tier (T4), you can spend 250 points per additional 1,000 stock — repeatable forever.
